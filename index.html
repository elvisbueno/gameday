2019-2020 JUNIOR ALL-AMERICAN GAMES

These three-day events at MLB Spring Training venus give standard baseball players the opportunity to train and play where Big Leaguers do. Participants will enjoy Opening Ceremonies, Individual Sports Performance Evaluation, Skills Competitions, and Pro-Style Combine.


HOW PLAYERS QUALIFY


Past Participants: Include Disney Team and Junior All-American Games Alumni automatically qualify and can register NOW.


Coach Nominations: Players can be nominated by their coach at any time via an online nomination form.


Scout Nominations: Game Day USA staff and scouts will be nominating outstanding players throughout the tournament season at Game Day USA tournaments and players will be selected and invited from Game Day USA ALL-Star Events.
